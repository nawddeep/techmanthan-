PROPOSED SOLUTION
YOUR IDEA

We built a unified AI dashboard for Udaipur city officials that ingests real-time simulated sensor data across
traffic, waste, and emergency domains. A FastAPI backend runs ML models every few seconds and serves
deployment recommendations to operators via a live Next.js dashboard

How It Solves the Problem

Key Features
•

Traffic Prediction: Random Forest model predicts high congestion at any of
8 Udaipur junctions based on hour, day, weather, and vehicle count-

•

Waste Overflow Prediction: ML model predicts bin overflow risk based on
area, population density, days since last collection, and current fill %-

•

Decision Engine: /system/decision API aggregates all three domains and
outputs specific actions (e.g., "Deploy traffic police at congested junction")-

•

Live City Map: Folium map of Udaipur showing traffic heatmap (red), waste
overflow markers (orange), emergency high-risk zones (blue)
